SS DRUM TANDOOR

The tandoor offered by us is available in customized options as per the client's specifications.
This tandoor is developed by using topmost grade raw material and leading techniques.
A drum tandoor is a clay pot fitted with a stainless steel barrel to reinforce the oven.
The pot inside the barrel is wrapped with thermal insulation, rock salts, ceramic powders, and glass wool so it burns very hot.
A hole is added at the bottom of the drum to remove ashes.
A wheel is also attached at the bottom of the drum for portability.
This tandoor oven is perfect for catering services or for baking outdoors.

Features:
  1. Long-lasting performance
  2. Easy to operate
  3. Low maintenance

Specifications:
  1. Size available: 30" x 30" x 36"
  2. Capacity: 20 chapati

Quality Policy

Quality Policy of Tandoor Fitting Specialists

At AZIZ J. MULLA, we have been at the forefront of Tandoor Fitting expertise since our inception in 1985. With a legacy of excellence spanning decades, we take pride in being the pioneers of Tandoor Fitting in Mumbai, India. As we transition into the next generation, our legacy continues to thrive under the dynamic leadership of our sons, who are propelling our venture to new heights.

  • 1. Tradition Meets Innovation: Built on a foundation of tradition and innovation, we blend time-honored Tandoor craftsmanship with modern techniques. Our legacy guides us, while our innovation ensures we remain relevant in a dynamic industry.
  • 2. Leadership in the Industry: Being the first to establish operations in Mumbai in 1985, we have consistently held a leadership position. Our reputation is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
  • 3. Expanding Footprint: Our services extend far beyond Mumbai. As proud suppliers of Tandoors and Tandoor Fitting Services, we cater to Maharashtra, Goa, and Gujarat states, ensuring our expertise reaches a wider audience.
  • 4. Customer-Centric Approach: Understanding that every Tandoor consumer seeks both quality and affordability, we have tailored our Tandoor Fitting Services to strike the perfect balance. Our mission is to provide high-quality Tandoors with impeccable fitting services, all at reasonable prices.
  • 5. Uncompromising Quality: Quality is the cornerstone of our operations. We recognize that a Tandoor's performance hinges on its construction, which is why we meticulously select the finest materials and adhere to stringent quality control measures.
  • 6. Skilled Craftsmanship: Our team comprises skilled craftsmen who are passionate about their work. Their expertise translates into Tandoors that stand the test of time and fitting services that ensure optimal performance.
  • 7. Continuous Improvement: We are committed to continuous improvement in all aspects of our business. From refining our processes to embracing emerging technologies, we strive for excellence in every endeavor.
  • 8. Environmental Responsibility: Recognizing our responsibility to the environment, we adopt sustainable practices in our operations. Minimizing our ecological footprint is a part of our commitment to the communities we serve.
  • 9. Customer Delight: We measure our success by the satisfaction of our customers. Exceeding expectations is our norm, and we are dedicated to ensuring that each client's experience with us is nothing short of exceptional.
  • 10. Empowered Workforce: Our success story is written by our dedicated team. We foster an environment that encourages creativity, growth, and a shared sense of purpose, allowing our employees to contribute to our vision.

At AZIZ J. MULLA, our quality policy encapsulates our ethos, our commitment to excellence, and our dedication to serving our customers with integrity and passion. For us, quality is not just a policy; it's a way of life that propels us forward on our journey of innovation and customer satisfaction.

Since 1985, AZIZ J. MULLA has established operations in Mumbai, India, and has grown into one of the most renowned Tandoor Fitting Specialists in the Industry.
